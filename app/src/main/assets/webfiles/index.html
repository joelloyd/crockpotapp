Welcome to CrockPot!

What is CrockPot?

CrockPot is a recipe-generating app that uses the ingredients you have in your fridge to produce tasty recipes!

How does it work?

Simply enter the ingredients you have, one by one and then press "Find me some recipes!"

The app will look up recipes using the ingredients you specified and return a list of recipes that you can view and save!
